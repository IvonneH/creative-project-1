Basketball

Pay attention.
Keep focus.
Eye on the ball.
I am the ball. It is me.
It thinks my thoughts, It is my head.
Don’t let me lose it.

Tangled Hair

My hair isn't dry,
But I'll be fine.
Maybe. It won't brush well
When wet, it gets tangled.
Forcing a brush through it is painful.
Why don't I have time to dry my hair?

I still get fed up with my hair
When it is dry.
If I forget to brush it, it could still be painful,
But I didn't forget so I'm fine.
Still, it continues to be tangled,
Though I brush it well.

Oh, well.
It’s not the fault of my hair
That it gets tangled.
Even when it’s dry.
It’s fine.
Not painful.

Painful?
Well,
Fine
Hair!
Dry
Tangled!

When I was little, my mom would leave it tangled.
But my dad would brush it, and comb it, which was painful.
Tangled hair every day? This old joke is dry.
I guess I've learned my lesson well.
I can't argue with my hair.
It always wins, and that's fine.

What really bugs me is when I can't fine-
D a hairbrush! Because hairdryer cords are all tangled,
To get ready for school I need to brush my hair.
To be late for such a reason would be painful.
If I’m late my grade wouldn't fair well.
Just because my hair won't dry!

A pathetic kind of painful.
I want my hair to look well,
So hair, please dry!
